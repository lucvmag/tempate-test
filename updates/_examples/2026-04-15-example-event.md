Last week we co-hosted a regional workshop on mobility data sharing with
five neighbouring municipalities, bringing together around forty
participants from city administrations, regional transport operators,
and two universities.

## Our role

We co-organised the event with the regional transport authority and led
the afternoon session on cross-city data integration. We presented our
shared data model (see our recent asset release) and ran a hands-on
exercise where participants explored the live integration between our
two partner cities.

## Takeaways

- Strong interest from three of the neighbouring cities in joining our
  integration work.
- Useful feedback on the data model — one participant pointed out a gap
  in how we handle shared administrative boundaries, which we will
  address in a future iteration.
- Two follow-up bilateral meetings already scheduled to discuss
  potential data-sharing agreements.

We will publish a short public summary of the workshop on the project
website in the coming weeks.
