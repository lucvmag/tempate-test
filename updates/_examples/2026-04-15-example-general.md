A short update on where we are with the sensor deployment side of our
pilot.

We submitted the technical specification for the air quality sensors to
our procurement office at the start of February. The process is now in
the supplier evaluation phase, which we expect to take another four to
six weeks.

In the meantime, the integration team is working on the data ingestion
pipeline so that we can connect sensors as soon as they arrive on site.
We are using the existing FIWARE Orion context broker we already had in
production for our open-data platform, with a new tenant for this pilot.

We will post a milestone update once the sensors are deployed and the
first data is flowing.
