We reached an important milestone this week: the first end-to-end data
integration between our two partner cities is now live and producing
results in our staging environment.

## What we did

Over the past six weeks, the technical teams in both cities worked on
harmonising the mobility data schemas. We adopted the NGSI-LD data
model for traffic sensors and aligned our timestamp conventions with
UTC across both platforms.

## What this enables

With the integration in place, we can now run the first cross-city
queries against a unified view of the mobility data. Our next step is
to validate the results against ground-truth measurements and, if all
goes well, move the integration into the production environment in the
coming weeks.

## Related work

The shared data model we used is published in our repository and linked
above under related assets. We expect to contribute it to the LDT4SSC
Assets and Services Repository once it has stabilised.
